    About Oga Mechanic

    MOBILE CAR REPAIR AND MAINTENANCE SERVICE

    Oga mechanic is a socially driven tech-based enterprise, which seeks to address the gap between the skilled, trained mechanics who offers affordable, quality services and the car owner who needs qualitative and easy-to-access maintenance services for their vehicle. Oga Mechanic connects both ends of the spectrum with a website and a mobile application on which a qualified and registered mechanic can be ordered from any part of Lagos and can be paid for his services through a wide variety of options at prices commensurate with the regular market.

    Car Repair

    Fixing of faulty cars by qualified mechanics

    Sale of Car Spare Parts

    Providing original spare part from genuine dealers

    Car Towing

    Moving of broken down vechicles to mechanic workshops.

    Car Diagnostics

    Conducting checks on cars with modern scanning equipments
